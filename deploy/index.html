Our mission is to design and produce tools that are of the highest quality to provide woodworkers and other craftsmen with the tools they need to create beautiful, functional, and lasting pieces.

Quality Assurance

Locally Sourced

We are stubbornly local and believe the best quality is right here in New England. We source all of our wood from tree farms in Maine and our metal casting from New England Foundries.

Strong Durability

Our products are built to last a lifetime because we use ductile iron for our blades and bronze alloy for our bolts. They are designed to withstand even the most demanding woodworking tasks.
